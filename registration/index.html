Registration

Registrations to GEOINFO 2025 are open until May 19, 2025. The conference fees are:

  • Undergraduate student: 120 BRL (Brazilian Real)
  • Post-graduate student: 170 BRL (Brazilian Real)
  • Professional: 300 BRL (Brazilian Real)

It is possible to pay the fee by deposit, bank transfer or PIX; Commitment Note for Brazilian federal institutions; or PayPal for foreign participants.

To proceed, fill out the registration form available here: https://selperbrasil.org.br/inscricao/geoinfo2025/
